Embedded Content

What it is:
You can include external media like videos, audio, maps, or PDFs inside your webpage to make them more interesting!

Why it’s used:

  • Enhance content with multimedia
  • Make websites more interactive

Basic examples:


<video controls>
  <source src="video.mp4" type="video/mp4">
</video>

<audio controls>
  <source src="audio.mp3" type="audio/mpeg">
</audio>

<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/dQw4w9WgXcQ" 
        width="300" 
        height="200">
</iframe>
