Tables

What it is:
Tables let you organize data into rows and columns, like a spreadsheet.
Why it’s used:

● Display data clearly (price lists, schedules, stats)

● Organize information that has a grid structure

Basic example: 


<table border="1">
    <tr>
        <th>Item</th>
        <th>Price</th>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <td>Apple</td>
        <td>$1</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <td>Banana</td>
        <td>$0.5</td>
    </tr>
</table>
