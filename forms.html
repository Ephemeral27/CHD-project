Forms

What it is:
Forms let users submit information to your website.

Why it’s used:

  • Contact forms
  • Signup/login pages
  • Surveys or feedback

Basic example:


<form>
  <label for="name">Name:</label>
  <input type="text" id="name" name="name" required>
  <button type="submit">Submit</button>
</form>
