Links & Media

What it is:

  • Links (<a>) let you navigate to other pages or websites
  • Media (<img>, <video>, <audio>) adds visual and audio content

Why it’s used:

  • Navigation and interactivity
  • Making content engaging

Example:


<a href="https://example.com" target="_blank">
  Visit Example
</a>

<img src="photo.jpg" alt="A cute cat">
